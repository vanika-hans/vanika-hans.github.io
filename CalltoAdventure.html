Call to Adventure

I was in fact called to be a leader. However, this call came from within, rather than having a little birdie or herald telling me to do so. This call rooted from my own instincts and personality, where I have learned and acknowledged that my greatest contribution to a group is generally directing the group. I have a tendency to plan out anything before I do it, and this has really made me realize that this sort of thinking is exactly what a leader does. However, as for challenges - I'm actually not too sure whether or whether not I should really become a self-proclaimed leader, or have someone else call me to that position. Every leadership position I have ever had has been a result of my initial effort; never have I been "nominated" to become a leader, but more assigned myself and efficiently created said task. So, the biggest challenging in accepting this call from within has been whether or whether not to truly consider it valid. However, my threshold guardians have been organizations that I have had a leadership role in (ex: SWE Outreach), where after becoming an active leader I have confirmed to both myself and my peers that I am fit to be a leader. However, there is yet so much to learn, and so much more adventure to experience.
